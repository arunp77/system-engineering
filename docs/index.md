# 🚀 Systems Engineering Learning Plan: A Comprehensive Guide in the Context of ESA/EUMTSAT/ESOC

Welcome to the System Engineering documentation.

---

## 📘 Executive Summary

This repo contains a structured, phase-by-phase learning roadmap for acquiring Systems Engineering (SE) competencies with a specific focus on the ESA/EUMTSAT/ESOC operational environment. The plan is designed for a professional with a background in remote sensing, Python programming, and SQL/databases who is transitioning into or expanding toward systems engineering roles in the European space sector.

- [EUMETSAT](https://www.eumetsat.int/) — the European Organisation for the Exploitation of Meteorological Satellites — operates a growing fleet of meteorological and environmental satellites from its headquarters in Darmstadt, Germany.
- [ESA](https://www.esa.int/) — the European Space Agency — is a European intergovernmental organisation of 22 member states dedicated to the exploration of space.
- [ESOC](https://www.esa.int/About_Us/ESOC) — European Space Operations Centre — is ESA’s centre for spacecraft operations. Its systems engineering roles demand a broad yet deep set of competencies spanning requirements management, ground segment architecture, model-based systems engineering (MBSE), verification and validation, data processing, and operations.
