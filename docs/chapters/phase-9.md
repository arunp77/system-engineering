## 9. Phase 9 — Practical Recommendations and Next Steps

### 9.1 Immediate Actions (First Month)

1. Download and begin reading ECSS-E-ST-10 and the NASA SE Handbook in parallel.
2. Install IBM DOORS trial or DOORS Next and complete the introductory tutorials.
3. Download Capella and work through the Air Traffic Management tutorial model.
4. Register on eumetsat.int and explore the technical documentation for MTG and EPS-SG.
5. Subscribe to EUMETSAT's EO Portal newsletter and follow their training event calendar.
