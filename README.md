# Systems Engineering Learning Plan: A Comprehensive Guide in the Context of EUMETSAT

- **Prepared for:** Personal Professional Development
- **Domain:** Space Systems Engineering — Meteorological Satellites
- **Location Context:** Frankfurt am Main / Darmstadt, Germany
- **Date:** April 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This repo contains a structured, phase-by-phase learning roadmap for acquiring Systems Engineering (SE) competencies with a specific focus on the EUMETSAT operational environment. The plan is designed for a professional with a strong background in remote sensing, Python programming, and SQL/databases who is transitioning into or expanding toward systems engineering roles in the European space sector.

EUMETSAT — the European Organisation for the Exploitation of Meteorological Satellites — operates a growing fleet of meteorological and environmental satellites from its headquarters in Darmstadt, Germany. Its systems engineering roles demand a broad yet deep set of competencies spanning requirements management, ground segment architecture, model-based systems engineering (MBSE), verification and validation, data processing, and operations. This report maps out how to acquire all of these competencies systematically over approximately twelve months.
